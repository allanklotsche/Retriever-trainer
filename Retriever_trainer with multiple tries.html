🖨 Saving as PDF on iPhone

Safari doesn't have a direct "Save as PDF" option, but here's the trick:

  1. Tap Continue to Print below
  2. When the print preview appears, use two fingers and pinch outward on the preview image
  3. This opens it as a PDF
  4. Tap the Share button (↑) and choose Save to Files
